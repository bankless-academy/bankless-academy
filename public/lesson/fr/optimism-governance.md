# Governance Seasons

Optimism takes an iterative approach to governance, using a calendar structure referred to as ‚ÄòSeasons‚Äô. By moving between periods of active governance and reflection, participants gain firsthand experience followed by digestion and discussion of current governance practices. The thoughtful feedback loops built across Optimism are incredibly important for finding best practices for `DAO` governance.
