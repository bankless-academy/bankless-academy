# How it Works

Letâ€™s bring it all together by looking under the hood. As a `peer-to-peer` network, Ethereum is made up of computers spread across the world. Using automated software, these `validators` process transactions, check the results of others to negate fraud, and generate `ether` rewards for their owners.
